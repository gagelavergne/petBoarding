At Just Paws we are here for you and your pet
 

Here at Just Paws we ensure your pet has time for themselves and one on one time with us. Whether that be sun bathing in the doggie pool, chasing the birds and squirrels, getting tummy rubs, playing fetch, or curled up on the couch.

We also guarantee a day filled with play and agility with other dogs. We have a fully fenced in yard, tons of toys, a doggie pool, a covered patio, and a separate section specifically for our smaller dogs!

So, what're you waiting for? You need a vacation to just pause and not worry about your pup and your dog needs a vacation where they can just pause and enjoy being...well a dog!
